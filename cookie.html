Purpose of the policy,

The validity and expiration periods of cookies and other similar trackers may vary depending on the lifetime set by us or the relevant provider. Some Trackers expire upon termination of the user's browsing session. Managing preferences and opting out

Users may manage Tracker related preferences and provide and withdraw consent for the Company to use Trackers and Cookies.

Certain devices allow users to manage Tracker and Cookie preferences directly from device settings. Also, when the use of Trackers is based on user consent, the user can provide or withdraw such consent by setting the preferences within the Cookie notice or in some cases by updating such preferences through the consent-preferences privacy options on the website. It may also be possible to use the browser or device features to delete previously stored Trackers, including those used to remember the initial consent preferences. Other Trackers in the browser's local memory may be cleared by deleting the browsing history.

With regard to any third-party trackers, you can manage your preferences via the related opt-out link (where provided), by using the means indicated in the third party's privacy policy, or by contacting the third party.
